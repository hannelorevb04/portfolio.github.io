Useless website - groep

In 2012, Tim Holman started his project The Useless Web, a collection of completely useless websites. In the Design 4 course, we were tasked with creating three XD versions of The Useless Web per group. The only criterion was that the idea had to be completely useless.

How did we approach it?

  • Exploration:

    In the exploration phase, we began with research on the original The Useless Web project and other similar useless websites. We analyzed the types of useless interactions and concepts that were popular, and brainstormed how we could contribute something unique.

  • Execution:

    We started by brainstorming and coding various ideas. We created a color picker, endless loader, and a page that simply displays that it's under construction. Then, we combined these to make a better whole.

Chicken or egg

What comes first: the chicken or the egg? That is the question we're trying to solve with our useless website. Every time you reload the site, you get another answer.
