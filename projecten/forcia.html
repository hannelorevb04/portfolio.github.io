Forcia

Forcia is the first project I developed in my career as an Experience Designer for the course Creation 1. The theme was to create a superhero app. After brainstorming, I decided to make an app that helps students organize their lives.

How did I proceed?

  • Theme:

    I started with researching the needs of students and analyzed existing solutions. Then I created wireframes and user flows to visualize the app's features.

  • Brainstorming:

    I generated various ideas and ultimately decided to develop an app that helps students organize their lives through a clear calendar and a competitive element.

  • Research:

    I conducted research on students' needs through surveys and interviews, and analyzed existing productivity apps to identify best practices.

  • Design:

    I created wireframes and user flows to visualize the key features and navigation of the app. After feedback and iterations, I refined these designs.

  • Prototype:

    I created an interactive prototype of Forcia using tools like Figma. This prototype was tested with users to evaluate usability and functionality.
