Google Doodle

For the course Advanced Frontend Development Integration, we were tasked to design a Google Doodle, I chose "Friends" as the theme.

How did we approach it?

  • Exploration:

    In the exploration phase, I began with thorough research on the theme "Friends" and the history of Google Doodles. I analyzed previous Doodles to understand the style and level of creativity required for a successful Doodle. Additionally, I studied various aspects of friendship and how they can be visually represented.

  • Execution:

    The execution phase revolved around developing and refining the design. I started with sketches and creating rough concepts, which I then refined into a final design. I made multiple sketches to explore different ideas. I focused on integrating iconic friendship elements into the Google logo. Colors and Style: I chose a color scheme that exudes cheerfulness and warmth, fitting for the theme of friendship. The style had to remain playful and recognizable, in line with the Google Doodle tradition. Digital Illustrations: Upon approval of the concept sketches, I developed the designs into digital illustrations. I used tools like Adobe Illustrator to create precise and appealing visuals. Animation (optional): For added dynamism, I considered adding simple animations, such as hands holding each other or smiling faces. This would make the Doodle even more lively.
