Google Doodle

For the course Advanced Frontend Development Integration, we were tasked to design a Google Doodle. I chose "Friends" as the theme.

How did we approach it?

  • Exploration: I started with thorough research on the theme "Friends" and the history of Google Doodles. This included analyzing past Doodles and exploring visual representations of friendship.
  • Execution: I developed the design with sketches and digital illustrations, using Adobe Illustrator. The final concept integrates cheerful colors, playful animations, and iconic friendship elements into the Google logo.
