Woordlogo - groep

In the Design 4 course, we were tasked with creating three word logos. The goal was to incorporate a visual reference to the meaning of each word in the logo design.

How did I proceed?

  • Exploration:

    In the exploration phase, I began by brainstorming visual references that could enhance the meaning of the words.

  • Execution:

    The execution phase involved developing the word logos, including choosing the font, applying necessary manipulations, and determining the colors and background.
